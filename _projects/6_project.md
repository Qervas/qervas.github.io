Led the development of an advanced autonomous fighting robot for the prestigious China Robotics Cup, showcasing cutting-edge robotics and AI capabilities:

- Programmed sophisticated algorithms in C for STM32 microcontroller
- Implemented state-of-the-art obstacle avoidance techniques
- Designed and coded intelligent attack strategies for engaging enemy robots
- Developed autonomous push-off techniques for dominating the fighting stage
- Clinched the third prize in the highly competitive 2019 China Robotics Cup
- Spearheaded C programming and intricate structure design, while coordinating with teammates on hardware assembly

This project exemplifies the perfect blend of software intelligence and mechanical engineering, pushing the boundaries of competitive robotics.

**Technologies:** STM32, C

**Timeline:** 2019

**Achievement:** Third Prize, 2019 China Robotics Cup
